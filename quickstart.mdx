## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A Meta Business account with admin access
- Access to your Facebook Page or Instagram account for channel connection
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari recommended)

</Callout>

Review these requirements to avoid setup issues.

## Create Your Account and Log In

Sign up for E-Smart360 in minutes to access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/signup` and enter your email, password, and business name.
    
    Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/login`.
    
    Enter your credentials to access the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="settings">
    Fill in your business details, including time zone and preferred language.
    
    Enable two-factor authentication for security.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your First Meta Channel

Link your Meta accounts to enable omnichannel messaging.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Facebook Messenger" icon="facebook">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Authorize App" icon="key">
        In the dashboard, go to Channels > Add Channel > Facebook Messenger.
        
        Click "Connect" and log in to your Meta Business account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Page" icon="globe">
        Choose your Facebook Page from the list.
        
        Grant permissions for messaging and pages access.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Instagram" icon="instagram">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Link Account" icon="link">
        Select Instagram in Channels and authorize via Meta login.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify" icon="check-circle">
        Confirm the connection status shows as active.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Test the connection by sending a message to your channel. It appears in the E-Smart360 inbox within seconds.
</Callout>

## Activate Basic AI Chat

Enable AI-powered responses for incoming messages.

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
  ````javascript
  // Example: Send initial setup webhook to E-Smart360
  const webhookData = {
    channel: "messenger",
    aiPrompt: "Greet users and offer product demo",
    enabled: true
  };

  fetch("https://api.example.com/v1/ai-chat/activate", {
    method: "POST",
    headers: { "Authorization": "Bearer YOUR_API_KEY", "Content-Type": "application/json" },
    body: JSON.stringify(webhookData)
  })
    .then(response => response.json())
    .then(data => console.log("AI Chat activated:", data));
  ````
  ````python
  import requests

  webhook_data = {
      "channel": "messenger",
      "ai_prompt": "Greet users and offer product demo",
      "enabled": True
  }

  response = requests.post(
      "https://api.example.com/v1/ai-chat/activate",
      headers={
          "Authorization": "Bearer YOUR_API_KEY",
          "Content-Type": "application/json"
      },
      json=webhook_data
  )
  print("AI Chat activated:", response.json())
  ````
</CodeGroup>

Replace `YOUR_API_KEY` with your generated key from the dashboard (Settings > API Keys).
